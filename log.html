Log

Time is short - so Strength Hub is committed to making it as simple as possible to record your training sessions; using your phone, tablet or computer.

You will be able to quickly record your training log - selecting which exercises you've, the intensity at which you did them and for how much volume. Leave your pen & paper log at home, and use our training log on your phone, in between sets and during rest periods.

Look back through your training log to see details of what you've done.
